What Is Feati Health And Safety?

Health And Safety

Health and safety have always been really important especially in this day and age and having a health and safety website is a must, especially that we are in a pandemic and that schools are thinking about opening and having face-to-face classes soon. The researchers realized that having a health and safety application for our university would significantly help the students and employees getting through this pandemic.

Brief background about the study

Health and safety are essential to every one of us. Even Abraham Maslow ranks it as the second most needing thing next to food and water. But what is Health and Safety? Cambridge defined at “the laws, rules, and principles that are intended to keep people safe from injury or disease at work and in public places. This simply means that things that we need to have or to do to be safe. But where does the health fits in? Simply put it as we are safe from injury and ailments, we are most probably healthy. Health and Safety during the pandemic This past year we experienced something that is life changing. Covid-19 came into our lives and it affected us all. Things cannot be normal anymore. And we need to conform to this “new normal”

Objective of the research

Health and safety have never been this hard to attain. Going outside is like risking your life to something invisible. One might argue that we’ve never been safer than before since the government has mandated us to stay inside as much as possible. But, are we truly we safe? What about the people that do need to go outside during this pandemic and What about the fear of you contracting the virus? This, is what FEATI Health and safety plans to answer. We know that there is a chance of us contracting the virus even if we stay inside all the time. We know that people are risking themselves all the time just for their livelihood. And as students we know the stress that all of us endure because we are uncertain of the future. And this is why we chose to create a web site. During this pandemic the reliance to the internet has never been matched before. According to DATAREPORTAL there is an estimate of 7.3 % increase in terms of internet users in the Philippines compared to January of 2020. This might not seem a lot but to think that Philippines is still in the process of urbanization. This is a lot. The transition in adapting to an online world has never been more needed till today. Since FEATI University one of the few universities leading the charge for innovation. The research team agreed upon leading the charge for adapting health and safety in an online environment. And what better way to do it than creating a website that would be accessible to all of us.
